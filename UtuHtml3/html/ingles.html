+ QUESTIONS IN PAST and PRESENT


  • PRESENT
      • Does + (he, she, it) + Verb infinitive
      • Do + (they, we, you, i) + Verb infinitive

        • Does he have a small family? ==> Yes, he does
        • Is Carlos Fernandez Uruguayan? ==> Yes, he is
        • Does he live abroad? ==> Yes, he does
        • Does he live with his wife? ==> Yes, he does
        • What does he do for his living? ==> Carlos works in I.T company

  • PAST
      • Did + (he, she, it, they, we, you, i) + Verb infinitive

        • Did they eat at an expensive restaurant? ==> Yes, they did
        • Did Carlos Fernandez and his family go to an expensive restaurant yesterday? ==> Yes, they did
        • Did they get up in the morgning? ==> Yes, they did
